Comleague Stations

Station 1: Ready, Set, Run

Participants will compete in a speed run programming contest where speed and accuracy are the keys to victory. The Game Master will provide coding problems, and players must solve the problem correctly and as fast as possible.

Station 2: Ready, Set, Run

Participants will compete in a typing contest that tests their speed, accuracy and keyboard skills.

Station 3: Trace, Inspect, and Execute

Teams will compete in a debugging contest, where the Game Master will provide a buggy program. Teams must identify and fix the errors as quickly as possible. The first team to successfully debug the programs wins.

Station 4: PC Assembly/Disassembly

Teams will compete in the PC Assembly and Disassembly Challenge, where they must disassemble and reassemble the components of a system unit within the given time. Facilitators will check for correct placement of components and determine who finishes first.

Station 5: LAN Pixels: The Wiring Battle

Participants will engage in a hands-on networking competition, racing to properly terminate an RJ45 Ethernet cable following the correct standards for Straight through cable and Crossover cable.

Station 6: Heritage meets Hardware

Participants will compete in either the Traditional or Digital Art Category. A description will be given by the faculty, which participants must illustrate within the allotted time.

Station 7: Mock-A-Thon: UI/UX edition

Teams will participate in a system and application design contest, creating mock start-ups that align with the United Nation Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through innovative UI/UX prototypes.
