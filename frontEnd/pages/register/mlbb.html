Important Guidelines

Read Before Registering
  • Registration Fee: 30.00 php per person
  • Participants must be enrolled in either the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology or the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science programs.
  • Each player must have their own mobile device with MLBB and Discord installed.
  • Team captains may submit the registration fee for the entire team
  • All participants must submit a valid document proving their current sector:
    • Students: Registration Form
    • Faculty: Faculty ID
    • Alumni: Official certificate or document verifying graduation
  • Teams are responsible for maintaining their own internet connection.
  • Each player may only play for one team.
  • After successful registration, Team Captains will be added to a Messenger group for updates.
