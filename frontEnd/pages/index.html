TECH Talks

A seminar focused on Cyber Security that provides participants with relevant knowledge related to the fields under the Department of Computer Studies, equipping students to be informed, aware, and inspired in addressing modern issues and threats.

ComLeague

ComLeague will feature a series of exciting activities that highlight the competitive spirit of the Department of Computer Studies. It will consist of seven stations, where each representative will take on different challenges that test their skills and knowledge. Each station will have its own set of rules and regulations.

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

An esports event that showcases the skills, teamwork, and strategic gameplay of participating students as they compete for victory and recognition in one of today’s most popular mobile games.

"Pixel Valorant" by Gas13

Source: gas13.deviantart.com

Valorant

An esports event that showcases tactical gameplay, precision, and team strategy as participating students compete for victory and recognition in this fast-paced 5v5 competitive shooter.

Cosplay Competition

This event aims to promote creativity, craftsmanship, self expression, and appreciation through cosplay. Cosplayers are encouraged to embody their chosen character through accurate costume design, performance and stage presentation. Cosplayers may portray any character of their choice or any video-game related characters.

Two Voices, One Song

This duet singing contest celebrates talent, confidence, and collaboration between students and faculty.
